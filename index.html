About Us

Decentralized Intellectual Property - D.I.P Platform

Decentralized Intellectual Property - D.I.P Platform DIP, or Decentralized Intellectual Property, is a new way of improving our world for the better. Through DIP, we aim to create a platform where any $FDBR holder can access and contribute to a library of artwork that is fully accessible to the public domain. This platform would also include the option to sell special NFT certificates that grant exclusive copyright rights to the wallet of the buyer.
